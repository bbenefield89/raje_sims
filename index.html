Raje Sims

l fell in love with the camera at the age of 13. Fortune would have it that my church had a full studio with a five camera set up.


Growing up I experimented with videography, gaining knowledge and inspiration from directors such as Spike LEE, Quentin TARANTINO and Alfred HITCHCOCK.


Over the years I have been in every position from camera to switcher and director. I knew this was not only my career but my true passion.


I went on to get my Bachelor's Degree in Communications Media Studies in 2009. I started my own production company servicing video for events such as weddings, school graduation, sports and parties.
